WHAT IS A PERSONAL LOAN?

Are your finances stopping you from fulfilling your dreams?

Confused as to how you can get out of your financial troubles? You don’t need to be!

A personal loan is an unsecured loan for individuals which can be used to manage various financial needs. The idea of a personal loan is to solve immediate financial contingencies quickly.

  • Loan without pledging any security
  • Loan starting from 1 Lac up to 10 Lacs
  • Loan Tenure of 12-60 months

When can you take a personal loan?

We live in the age of disruption and technology is reshaping the new normal every decade for us. In these uncertain times, we will have to constantly upgrade our knowledge and skill-set to prevent being rendered redundant or explore emerging career options that offer good growth and financial gains. Personal loans help you explore your education options for yourself and reskill to gain better opportunities. If you are for some unforeseen reason unable to pay fee of your child’s school or college education, then also, feel free to reach out to us and we would help.

Are you in an emergency with a hefty bill and do not know what to do next? Reach out to us for an emergency medical loan and we will ensure that you get it on time without hassle. This is an unplanned and unexpected emergency when personal loans can help to immediately address your funds without denting your bank savings. The loan can be repaid in easy EMIs without hurting your financial well-being. Given the nature of requirement, we try to keep the loan process as easy, smooth and hassle-free as possible for our customers

They say that we all intend to marry once in our lives and for a lifetime. Our Indian weddings are a festival, and no expenses are spared by grooms, brides or families to ensure that everyone remembers the wedding and its functions. Personal loans can help you add an extra zing to your wedding or help you plan your dream wedding. Personal loans start at reasonable rates to let you fulfill your desires.

Travel helps us expand our minds and horizons and offer a fresh perspective to our lives. Personal loans for travel are increasingly being availed to help people visit places they once could only dream. Your perfect and best vacation spots need not wait till tomorrow as our personal loans help you take them today. Apply and sip your comfort drink while we do the heavy-lifting on your behalf.
