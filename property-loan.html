WHAT IS LOAN AGAINST PROPERTY

How about your own property helping you fulfill your dreams? Make the best use of your property by getting the best value in times of financial need!

Loan against property is a secured loan which means you will have to hold your property mortgage to get a loan. This type of loan can be availed by both salaried and self-employed borrowers.

  • Secured Loan by pledging asset/property.
  • Loan starting from 7 Lacs upto 50 Lacs for salaried and upto 75 Lacs for self- employed.
  • Loan Tenure of 5-10 years.

Features of Loan Against Property

Get loan amount for salaried and self-employed upto 75 Lacs for a maximum tenure of 10 years at attractive interest rates.

Get funds easily with our quick, hassle-free and transparent process.

Submit minimal documentation like Age, Identity & address proof, Income documents, Bank statements and Property documents.

Get highest value for your property mortgaged at low interest rate, be it residential or commercial in nature.

Advantages of Loan Against Property

Flexibility in mortgaging property of your choice

Longer loan tenure
